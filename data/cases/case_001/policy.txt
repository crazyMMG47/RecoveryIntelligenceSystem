Kaiser Permanente Rehabilitation Policy

Coverage Rules:
- Standard physical therapy coverage: 1 session per week without additional authorization
- Additional sessions require physician justification and review

Continuity of Care:
- Rehabilitation protocols may vary across Kaiser locations
- Consistency of care is not guaranteed across facilities

Patient Responsibility:
- Patient adherence to prescribed rehabilitation is a key factor in determining eligibility for extended care
- Missed or incomplete rehabilitation programs may negatively impact approval decisions

Revision Surgery Policy:
- Coverage for revision ACL surgery requires documented failure of a structured and completed rehabilitation program

Additional Constraints:
- Requests for extended PT may be denied if prior non-adherence is documented
- Insufficient evidence of completed rehabilitation may result in denial of both extended PT and surgical authorization

Conclusion:
Further treatment approval is contingent on documented adherence and completion of prior rehabilitation protocols.
